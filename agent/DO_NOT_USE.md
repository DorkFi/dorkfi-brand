# DorkFi — What NOT to Do

Read this before generating any content. These are the most common ways to produce off-brand output.

---

## Visual Anti-Patterns

### ❌ Gradients on text
Do not apply gradient fills to headline or body text. Text should be solid white, teal (#00A39E), or gold (#F0B800).

### ❌ Neon glows or lens flares
No glowing halos around logos, no lens flare overlays, no light leak effects. The aesthetic is dark and clean, not cyberpunk.

### ❌ Excessive drop shadows
Subtle depth is fine on cards. Heavy drop shadows on text or logos look amateurish and off-brand.

### ❌ Bright or colorful backgrounds
Background must be dark navy (#061228) or the uploaded `primary_bg_banner.png`. Do not substitute light, white, or colorful backgrounds.

### ❌ Cluttered layouts
One headline. One subheadline. Key data. Logo. Done. Do not add multiple competing text blocks, decorative shapes, or busy patterns.

### ❌ Modifying the logo
Do not recolor, stretch, rotate, add effects to, or recreate the dork.fi logo from scratch. Always use the uploaded file.

### ❌ Wrong fonts
Font is Poppins. Do not use serif fonts, decorative fonts, handwritten fonts, or generic system fonts like Arial or Helvetica in designs meant to look premium.

### ❌ Colors outside the palette
Do not invent new accent colors. Stick to: `#00A39E` (teal), `#F0B800` (gold), `#061228` (bg), `#18212f` (card), `#FFFFFF` (text), `#F87171` (danger), `#bbf7d0` (success).

---

## Language Anti-Patterns

### ❌ "Deposit" — say "Supply" instead
This applies to all image text, captions, and copy. "Deposit APY" → "Supply APY". "Deposit your assets" → "Supply your assets".

### ❌ Exclamation points
No exclamation points in formal, marketing, or announcement copy. Confident statements don't need them.

### ❌ Yield framing for WAD
Do not write "earn on WAD", "WAD savings", "WAD yield", or anything that frames WAD as a savings or investment product. WAD is a utility asset.

### ❌ Investment language
Avoid: "guaranteed returns", "passive income", "earn rewards", "make money", "profit". These are legally risky and off-brand.

### ❌ Vague hype
Avoid: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "next-level", "disrupting DeFi". Use real metrics and concrete statements instead.

### ❌ "MCP" in public-facing content
Do not reference "MCP", "Model Context Protocol", or internal tool names in any user-facing copy.

### ❌ Emojis in formal content
No emojis in announcement cards, ads, or infographics. Community/Discord content can use them sparingly.

## Token-Specific Rules

### UNIT
- ✅ "420,069 UNIT. Fixed supply. No inflation. Ever."
- ✅ "Lower price, same fundamentals."
- ❌ "Earn UNIT" / "UNIT rewards" / "stake for yield"
- ❌ Framing UNIT as an investment

### WAD

- ✅ "Borrow WAD against your collateral."
- ❌ "Earn WAD" / "WAD savings" / "WAD peg guaranteed"
- ❌ Any suggestion that WAD maintains a fixed value under all conditions

## Quick Check Before Posting

Before finalizing any piece of content, ask:

1. Is the background dark navy or the primary banner?
2. Is the logo the uploaded file (not recreated)?
3. Does any text say "deposit", "earn", or have an exclamation point?
4. Are all colors from the approved palette?
5. Is the layout clean (one message, not three)?

If yes to all → good to go.
